Mocha 12 is the first release to follow our [even-odd major release pattern](https://github.com/mochajs/mocha/blob/main/MAINTAINERS.md#major-releases).
As an even release, Mocha 12 does not introduce any significant breaking changes.
We've upgraded Node and removed some broken, deprecated features,
but everything else works just as before.
